Daily Bible

This website helps you read the Bible in a year according to the OSB planner.

How to use this tool: Whenever you want to begin the reading schedule, click on Day 1.
Then, finish reading it (ideally within a day), and press the "next" button to get tomorrow's assignment.
Continue this every day to finish the entire Bible in a year!
Use the textbox or the "Save Progress" button to keep track of your studies.

A suggested prayer for reading the Bible.

See the project page for more details: https://github.com/digitalconfucius/daily-bible.






Reporting bugs: the easiest way to report a bug is to create an issue on the project page. Thank you!

You can also track your progress on Goodreads by adding the KJV with Apocrypha and updating your "pages read" by 4 every day.
