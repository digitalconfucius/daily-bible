Daily Bible

This website helps you read the Bible in a year according to the OSB planner.

How to use this tool: Whenever you want to begin, start on Day 1.
Finish reading it, and press the "next" button to get tomorrow's assignment.
Continue this every day to read the entire Bible in a year!
See also: a suggested prayer for reading the Bible.






See the project page for more details: https://github.com/digitalconfucius/daily-bible.

Reporting bugs: the easiest way to report a bug is to create an issue on the project page or message me on Twitter. Thank you!

You can also track your progress on Goodreads by adding the KJV with Apocrypha and updating your "pages read" by 4 every day.
